About Myself ✦

I'm Claire Kim, a passionate Mechanical Engineering student at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). I have a deep interest in CAD, digital fabrication, and robotic systems.

When I'm not studying core mechanical concepts, I'm actively applying them as the Mechanical Engineering Lead for Theta Tau's Aeris project and as a Chassis Engineer for X1 Robotics.

I thrive in hands-on environments—from FDM 3D printing and precise machining to electromechanical assembly—and I am always eager to solve complex design challenges.
